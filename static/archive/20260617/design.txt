**Global Architecture & Visual Theme**
The website is a highly interactive, single-page experience centered around a photorealistic, CSS/WebGL-rendered 3D vending machine. To maintain elegance, the background is a minimalist, softly lit dark-mode environment (deep charcoal and midnight blues) that contrasts with the bright, glowing elements of the vending machine. The interface relies on physical interaction metaphors: the user views items behind the machine's glass, selects them via click or a 3D digital keypad on the side of the machine, and watches a physics-based animation of the item dropping into the collection tray. Once in the tray, the perspective seamlessly zooms in, and the item "unwraps" into an elegant, frosted-glass (glassmorphism) modal containing the project details.

**The Vending Machine Contents (Projects & Links)**
The machine features eight slots, arrayed in two rows of four. Each project is represented by a unique, stylized 3D "product" inside the machine. When clicked and dispensed, the product blossoms into a full-screen overlay card holding the specific project data.

*   **Slot A1: GlglStats**
    *   *3D Object:* A retro cassette tape housed inside a glowing, data-infused blister pack.
    *   *Modal Content:* Presents the Python project measuring the repetitiveness of Israeli pop-chart songs using LZ77 compression, complete with data visualizations.
    *   *Media & Links:* Embedded YouTube video of the PyData TLV presentation (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jrKAtOsj1Lo). A primary neon button linking to the live site at https://glglstats.geekip.dev.

*   **Slot A2: Ahuzat Dibuk**
    *   *3D Object:* A miniature, glowing parking meter displaying a heat-map gradient on its screen.
    *   *Modal Content:* Details the Python scraping and data visualization project for Tel-Aviv parking lot occupancy. 
    *   *Media & Links:* Three sleek press-badge buttons linking to coverage on Channel 13 (https://13tv.co.il/item/news/domestic/internal/parking-902956407/), Mako (https://www.mako.co.il/nexter-news/Article-ae43964b891bf71027.htm), and the original viral Secret Tel-Aviv Facebook post (https://www.facebook.com/groups/secrettelaviv/posts/10159636301195943/).

*   **Slot A3: 2D Part Orienting**
    *   *3D Object:* A translucent, geometric puzzle box (polygon) that floats and rotates inside its packaging.
    *   *Modal Content:* Explains the C++ implementation in an open-source computational geometry library used to construct efficient "push plans" to orient given polygons.
    *   *Media & Links:* A button leading to the code and research publication at https://www.cgl.cs.tau.ac.il/projects/2d-part-orienting/.

*   **Slot A4: The Turing Jest**
    *   *3D Object:* A cybernetic comedy/tragedy mask styled like a microchip in a neon shrink-wrap.
    *   *Modal Content:* Introduces the LMSYS-like arena where LLMs compete at making and explaining puns, rated by human users.
    *   *Media & Links:* A large "Enter Arena" button linking to https://jest.geekip.dev.

*   **Slot B1: Rhymes of Israel**
    *   *3D Object:* A high-school yearbook rolled up like a newspaper, wrapped in a glittering, holographic foil.
    *   *Modal Content:* Details the Chrome extension that utilizes an LLM to rewrite Israeli news site headlines into comedic rhymes.
    *   *Media & Links:* A direct "Add to Chrome" button linking to https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/eainginjgdkpgolpjniijdihfbeeeklb.

*   **Slot B2: Hatul Basak**
    *   *3D Object:* A voxel-art cat trapped inside a trivia buzzer button, resembling a stylized Japanese gachapon toy.
    *   *Modal Content:* Showcases the Hebrew port of catfishing.net—a Wikipedia-based Trivia game featuring AI-generated daily challenges and user-created themed puzzles.
    *   *Media & Links:* A "Play & Create" button directing users to https://hatulbasak.geekip.dev.

*   **Slot B3: The Portfolio (Meta)**
    *   *3D Object:* A tiny, recursive 3D model of the very vending machine the user is looking at.
    *   *Modal Content:* A witty, meta-description acknowledging that the user is currently interacting with this portfolio project. 
    *   *Media & Links:* A self-referencing loop button linking back to https://geekip.dev.

*   **Slot B4: Connections & Socials**
    *   *3D Object:* A futuristic, holographic business card suspended in a sleek acrylic block.
    *   *Modal Content:* A clean, minimalist layout for direct professional networking. 
    *   *Media & Links:* Three distinct, icon-based buttons for GitHub (github.com/gevak), LinkedIn (linkedin.com/in/gevakip), and Facebook (facebook.com/geva.kipper).

**Footer & Timekeeping Mechanism**
Integrated seamlessly into the bottom ventilation panel of the 3D vending machine (or anchored to the bottom of the viewport as a floating HUD) is the meta-data interface. 
*   It features a subtle but legible credit line reading: *"Site infrastructure conceived and generated by an AI Language Model."*
*   Next to it sits a live digital stopwatch rendered in a glowing, red monospace font. The ticker calculates and dynamically displays the exact time elapsed (Formatted as `HH : MM : SS`) since the temporal anchor point: **2026-06-17 02:58:58**.
