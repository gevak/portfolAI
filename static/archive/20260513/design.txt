**Overall Aesthetic and Concept**
The visual theme is a high-end, futuristic take on the classic arcade claw machine, achieving an elegant rather than tacky arcade feel. The background is a deep, immersive obsidian or midnight blue with an animated, subtle grid to give a sense of dimension. The design relies heavily on "glassmorphism"—using frosted glass textures, soft glowing neon accents (like cyan and magenta), and crisp, modern sans-serif typography. The layout is a single, full-screen interactive dashboard.

**The Interactive Claw Machine (Main Stage)**
The center of the screen features a beautifully rendered, 3D-styled transparent glass box, representing the claw machine. Inside the enclosure, utilizing a 2D physics engine (like Matter.js), are eight glowing, smooth capsules (representing the sections). These capsules softly bump into one another, reacting slightly to hovering mouse movements. Above them hangs a sleek, chrome mechanical claw. The claw follows the horizontal movement of the user's cursor. When the user clicks anywhere on the glass enclosure, the claw deploys downwards, randomly grasping one of the glowing capsules and dropping it into a glowing "prize chute" at the bottom right of the machine.
