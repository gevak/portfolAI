**Overall Visual Concept & Interaction Mechanic**
The fundamental interaction of the website is an "Erase-to-Reveal" mechanic. When the user loads the page, the entire viewport is covered by an HTML5 Canvas overlay. This overlay mimics an intricately sketched, vintage 1858 US Patent document on aged, sepia-toned paper, specifically detailing the March 30 invention of the pencil with an attached eraser. The cursor is replaced by a custom graphic of a classic pink rubber eraser. As the user clicks and drags across the screen, the canvas acts as a scratch-off mask (using `globalCompositeOperation="destination-out"`), revealing the modern, vibrant portfolio underneath. As the user erases, tiny CSS-animated "eraser shavings" fall to the bottom of the screen. Once 40% of the canvas is erased, the remaining vintage overlay shatters into graphite dust and fades out, leaving the user with a pristine, highly modern digital interface. 

**Color Palette & Typography**
The revealed interface contrasts sharply with the vintage overlay. The background is a sleek, deep charcoal matte (representing graphite), accented with electric "Eraser Pink" (#FF6680) and crisp white for text. Typography pairs a futuristic, geometric sans-serif for headings (e.g., Space Grotesk) with a highly legible, elegant sans-serif for body descriptions (e.g., Inter).
