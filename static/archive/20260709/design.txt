The page background features an interconnected WebGL hexagonal grid—a structural integrity field—that subtly undulates and breathes, simulating a living cybernetic organism. 

The Header section is framed as a "Primary Cortical Node" interface. It sits asymmetrically in the upper-left corner, bordered by a curved titanium UI element reminiscent of Seven of Nine’s ocular implant. The text is crisp and futuristic (using fonts like *Orbitron* and *Rajdhani*). The main title reads prominently: "Designation: Geva Kipper."
