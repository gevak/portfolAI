Above them hover retro-futuristic HP and Status bars. Bridging the gap between the battle stage and the interactive panels below is the primary RPG text box. It acts as a dynamic typewriter, announcing: "Geva Kipper has entered the encounter! Currently wielding infrastructure spells as a Software Engineer at Google."
