**Initial State: The "Digital Handshake" Minigame (Landing Screen)**
Upon loading, the visitor is greeted by a pitch-black, full-screen canvas. In the center floats a minimalist, glowing wireframe of a hand, rendered in a sleek cyan and deep purple gradient (representing "The Host"). The user’s cursor is replaced by a matching, smaller wireframe hand (representing "The Client"). Clean, monospace text pulses at the bottom: `"Initiate Handshake Protocol to Establish Connection."` 
To unlock the site, the user must drag their cursor into the host’s hand. Upon overlapping, the hands visually lock. The user is prompted to drag the mouse up and down rhythmically three times. With each "shake," a status bar progresses: `[SYN] -> [SYN-ACK] -> [ACK]`. Upon completion, a crisp sound effect chimes, the screen flashes a soft white, and the wireframe hands shatter into hundreds of geometric particles that fall away to reveal the portfolio beneath.

**Overall Layout & Aesthetic**
The portfolio utilizes a highly modern "Bento Box" grid layout. The aesthetic is "Elegant Dark Mode Cyberpunk"—a deep charcoal background (#121212) accented by frosted glass panels (Glassmorphism), subtle neon drop-shadows, and smooth CSS fade-in animations. The grid is asymmetrical, seamlessly adapting to desktop and mobile views. 

**Hero Section (Top)**
