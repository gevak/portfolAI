# Spotify-Inspired Portfolio Design: "Geva Kipper's Dev Mixtape"

## Overall Aesthetic & Layout

The page uses a dark theme with a near-black background (#121212) reminiscent of Spotify's signature look. The primary accent color is a vibrant electric green (#1DB954) used sparingly for interactive elements, hover states, and key highlights. Secondary accents include soft purple (#B388FF) and coral (#FF6B6B) to differentiate project "genres." Typography combines a bold sans-serif for headers (Circular-style or Montserrat) with a clean sans-serif for body text. The entire page has subtle grain texture overlay for depth.
