The overall layout is framed to mimic the physical facade of an elegant, futuristic, glass-front vending machine. The background utilizes a dark, charcoal-blue gradient (reminiscent of deep night) with internal, cold-white and amber LED strip lighting illuminating the "interior" of the machine. The website relies heavily on scroll-linked parallax animations, giving the illusion that scrolling down lowers a robotic elevator arm past various lit shelves.

**Header: The Vending Machine Marquee**
The top of the page features a sleek, glowing OLED-style digital panel seamlessly integrated into the machine's casing. 
*   **Primary Text:** "Geva Kipper", styled in a sharp, modern sans-serif typeface.
*   **Status Text:** Just below, a clean sentence: "Currently engineering scalable solutions as a Software Engineer at Google."
*   **The Ticker tape:** A continuously sliding horizontal dot-matrix ticker reads: "SYSTEM NOTIFICATION: This website's layout and interface is re-designed each day by AI and is thus ever-changing." 

**The Viewport & Interaction (The Glass Window)**
The main body of the single-page site is the glass window of the machine. Using JavaScript and CSS, the projects are laid out on metallic, frosted-glass shelves. Next to each item is a glowing silver coil. As the user scrolls, the viewpoint moves down the vertical track. Hovering over any item causes the coil to rotate slightly and the item to glow. Clicking an item "dispenses" it—triggering a smooth CSS animation where the item drops off the bottom of the screen, and immediately expands into a beautiful, glassmorphism modal representing the "Nutritional Facts" or "Product Packaging" containing the project details and links.

Although conceptualized as software, each project is visually represented as a distinct, high-end physical product resting in the machine:

At the very bottom of the page sits a heavy, dark-metal delivery flap (the dispense tray). 
*   Embossed directly onto the metal texture of the flap is the credit: "System Architecture and UI Generation by an AI Model."
