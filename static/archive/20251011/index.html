GlglStats

In this chapter, our hero created a tool to illuminate the hidden patterns of speech...

GlglStats is an interactive visualization platform that analyzes and displays statistical patterns in language usage. This project emerged from a fascination with how we communicate and the repeating patterns that emerge in natural speech and text.

The tool processes text data to reveal frequency distributions, common patterns, and linguistic quirks through beautiful, interactive visualizations. It has been presented at data science conferences and continues to help researchers and language enthusiasts explore the mathematical beauty hidden in everyday communication.

Ahuzat Dibuk

In this chapter, our hero uncovered the mysteries of parking in the ancient city...

Ahuzat Dibuk (The Haunted Estate) is a data-driven investigation into Tel Aviv's parking patterns. Using computer vision and data analysis, this project mapped parking availability across the city from a bird's-eye view, revealing the invisible rhythms of urban life.

The project gained significant media attention, being featured on national television and news websites, sparking conversations about urban planning and the creative use of public data to solve everyday problems.

2D Part Orienting

In this chapter, our hero ventured into the realm of geometric puzzles...

This research project, conducted at Tel Aviv University's Computer Graphics Lab, explores algorithms for optimally orienting 2D parts for manufacturing and packing. The work combines computational geometry, optimization theory, and practical manufacturing constraints.

The research has applications in industrial manufacturing, material waste reduction, and automated production systems. The algorithms developed can significantly improve efficiency in cutting, printing, and fabrication processes.

The Turing Jest

In this chapter, our hero created a game where humans and machines engage in playful deception...

The Turing Jest is an interactive game that playfully explores the boundaries between human and artificial intelligence. Inspired by the classic Turing Test, this project invites players to guess whether they're interacting with a human or an AI, all while maintaining a lighthearted and entertaining atmosphere.

The game serves both as entertainment and as an educational tool, helping people understand the current capabilities and limitations of AI through direct interaction and playful experimentation.

Rhymes of Israel

In this chapter, our hero brought the wisdom of ancient poets to the modern scribe...

Rhymes of Israel is a Chrome extension that helps writers and poets find rhymes in Hebrew. Drawing from the rich tradition of Hebrew poetry and literature, this tool makes the art of versification more accessible to modern creators.

The extension integrates seamlessly into the browser, providing instant rhyme suggestions and poetic inspiration wherever you write. It's a bridge between ancient linguistic traditions and contemporary digital creativity.
